## What is Prompting?

A prompt tells the AI what to generate. The more detail you add, the better the results!

**Basic Prompt**:  
`a pony`

**Improved Prompt**:  
`a pastel-colored unicorn with flowing hair, standing in a magical field under the moonlight`
